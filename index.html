Our Story

Where Every Dish
Tells a Story

Founded in 2023 in the heart of Phnom Penh, Mhub khmer was born from a family passion for preserving authentic Khmer culinary heritage. Our chefs use centuries-old recipes with the finest local ingredients — fresh herbs, Kampot pepper, and lemongrass from trusted local farms.

Fresh Local Ingredients

Sourced daily from Phnom Penh markets and trusted Cambodian farms.

Traditional Cooking Methods

Clay pots, open flames, and slow-cooked broths just like grandmother made.

Award-Winning Cuisine

Recognized by Cambodia Tourism Board three years running.
